# VillageTale Milestones

This document outlines the development roadmap from Alpha 1 through the 1.0 release.

## Alpha 1 - Goodbye Yellow Brick Road

**Goal:** Establish a self-sustaining village with core work mechanics.

### Included Features
- Town Hall and village territory management
- Mayor's Ledger for managing villages
- Zone system with boundary visualization (F3+V toggle)
- Village hiring system (convert vanilla villagers)
- Hunger system requiring villagers to eat
- Daily schedules (wake, eat, work, socialize, sleep)
- Real crafting with materials from shared storage
- Tool usage and durability
- Recipe teaching system
- Production limits for crafters

### Included Professions
- Cook - Prepares food from raw ingredients
- Farmer - Tills soil, plants seeds, harvests crops
- Forester - Cuts and plants trees
- Herder - Breeds animals, shears sheep, captures wandering livestock
- Butcher - Slaughters animals and processes meat/leather
- Fisher - Catches fish at water sources
- Carpenter - Crafts wooden tools and construction materials
- Leatherworker - Crafts leather armor, books, leads, and items

### Included Zone Types
- Kitchen - Where cooks prepare food
- Farmland - Where farmers grow crops
- Forest - Where foresters harvest and plant trees
- Pen - Where herders manage animals
- Butchery - Where butchers process animals
- Dock - Where fishers catch fish
- Woodshop - Where carpenters craft wooden items
- Tannery - Where leatherworkers craft leather goods
- Home - Where villagers sleep and live
- Storage - Communal storage for village resources
- Town Hall - Heart of the village

## Alpha 2 - Durban Deep

**Goal:** Add stats and metal production chain.

### New Features

**Stats System:**
- Strength - Carrying capacity, mining speed, tree-chopping speed, weapon damage
- Endurance - Work speed, break frequency, hunger consumption rate
- Intelligence - Recipe capacity, crafting success rate (low Intelligence = wasted materials)
- Stats affect all existing profession performance

### New Professions
- Miner - Extract underground resources
- Smelter - Process ores and smelt materials
- Blacksmith - Craft metal tools, weapons, and armor

### New Zone Types
- Mine - Where miners extract underground resources
- Foundry - Where smelters process ores
- Smithy - Where blacksmiths craft metal items

## Alpha 3 - Tiny Dancer

**Goal:** Add happiness, relationships, and professions to improve village atmosphere

### New Features

**Happiness System:**
- Housing quality affects villager contentment
- Village atmosphere (decorations, flowers, lighting) improves mood
- Population balance (overcrowding causes stress)
- Grief from recent deaths affects entire village
- Happiness impacts work efficiency, movement speed, and family planning

**Grief & Mourning:**
- Villagers react emotionally to deaths of friends and family
- Grief impacts happiness levels

**Relationships:**
- Organic friendships develop from frequent interactions
- Relationship strength affects grief responses and happiness

**Social Spaces:**
- Tavern zone for villagers to gather and socialize in evenings
- Improves village happiness and strengthens relationships

### New Professions
- Mason - Process stone into decorative blocks
- Beekeeper - Manage beehives and harvest honey
- Gardener - Cultivate flowers and provide dyes
- Chandler - Create torches, lanterns, candles, and light sources

### New Zone Types
- Masonry - Where masons process stone
- Apiary - Where beekeepers manage hives
- Garden - Where gardeners cultivate flowers
- Chandlery - Where chandlers create light sources
- Tavern - Where villagers gather and socialize

## Alpha 4 - The Emperor's New Clothes

**Goal:** Add quests and commerce

### New Features

**Dialogue & Interaction System:**
- Direct conversation UI for talking to villagers (separate from Ledger)
- Villagers reveal quests through conversation
- Access villager inventory by asking them directly
- Ledger tracks known quests but discovery happens through interaction

**Quest System:**
- Personal requests from villagers (beds, housing, job changes)
- Village projects (taverns, decorations, infrastructure improvements)
- Dynamic quests based on villager needs and happiness
- Quests discovered through dialogue, tracked in Ledger

**Commerce:**
- Traveling Merchants visit Town Hall with quests from distant villages
- Merchant profession for selling village surplus goods

### New Professions
- Merchant - Sells village surplus goods for emeralds

### New Zone Types
- Market - Where merchants sell goods

## Alpha 5 - Your Song

**Goal:** Add romance, families, and the circle of life and death

### New Features

**Romance & Marriage:**
- Friendships evolve into romantic partnerships (requires Relationships from Alpha 3)
- Marriage requests for adjoining beds
- Couples move in together

**Families & Children:**
- Married couples have children
- Children exist as smaller entities with distinct behaviors
- Children learn through play activities:
  - Playing tag builds Endurance
  - Climbing trees builds Strength
  - Hide and seek builds Intelligence
- Apprenticeship system - children watch adults work and learn recipes
- Children choose new activities every few minutes, naturally developing different skills

**Complete Life Cycle:**
- Age tracking from birth through death
- Visual changes as villagers age (child/adult/elder appearance)
- Children mature into productive adults who seek their own homes
- Elders eventually die of old age
- Full generational progression

### New Professions
- Child - Young villagers who play, learn, and grow

## Beta 1 - Daniel
**Goal:** Add the ability for villagers to defend their home and a way to remember those who came before

### New Features
- Villagers can take up arms and join the Militia during Raids
- Villages can be raided like Vanilla villages can

### New Professions
- Guard - Patrol and defend in melee combat
- Archer - Patrol and defend from range
- Priest - Tend to the ill or wounded and bury the dead

### New Zones
- Guard Post - Where Guards and Archers patrol or stand guard
- Cemetery - Where the Priest buries the dead

## 1.0 - I'm Still Standing
**Goal:** Polish and guidebook

### New Features
- A guidebook exists in game with an explanation of all professions and zones and how to manage your village

## Beyond 1.0

Additional professions, compatibility features, and advanced systems will be considered after 1.0 is stable. See [PROFESSIONS.md](PROFESSIONS.md) for more information.
