# VillageTale Professions

A living document exploring which professions belong in VillageTale and why.

## Confirmed Professions (YES)

### Archer
- **What they do:** Defends from range, maintains distance from threats, equips bows/crossbows
- **Where they work:** Guard Post zone, Patrol Route zone

### Beekeeper
- **What they do:** Manages beehives, harvests honey/honeycomb, maintains bee populations
- **Where they work:** Apiary zone

### Blacksmith
- **What they do:** Crafts all metal items - tools, weapons, and armor - based on taught recipes
- **Where they work:** Smithy zone

### Butcher
- **What they do:** Slaughters animals from pens, processes into raw meat, leather, and other materials
- **Where they work:** Butchery zone

### Chandler
- **What they do:** Creates light sources - torches, lanterns, candles, redstone lamps, copper bulbs
- **Where they work:** Chandlery zone

### Cook
- **What they do:** Prepares food from raw ingredients based on taught recipes
- **Where they work:** Kitchen zone

### Farmer
- **What they do:** Tills soil, plants seeds, harvests crops, maintains farmland
- **Where they work:** Farmland zone

### Fisher
- **What they do:** Fishes at water sources, provides fish as alternative food source
- **Where they work:** Dock zone

### Gardener
- **What they do:** Cultivates and breeds flowers, maintains flower gardens, provides dyes
- **Where they work:** Garden zone

### Guard
- **What they do:** Patrols village, engages threats in melee combat, equips swords/shields
- **Where they work:** Guard Post zone, Patrol Route zone

### Herder
- **What they do:** Breeds animals, shears sheep, milks cows, plucks feathers, captures wandering animals
- **Where they work:** Pen zone

### Leatherworker
- **What they do:** Crafts leather armor, books, item frames, leads, and modded leather items (backpacks, satchels)
- **Where they work:** Tannery zone

### Forester
- **What they do:** Cuts trees, plants trees
- **Where they work:** Forest zone

### Mason
- **What they do:** Processes stone into decorative blocks (bricks, polished stone, chiseled variants), crafts stone construction materials
- **Where they work:** Masonry zone

### Miner
- **What they do:** Mines resources by digging into the earth (specific mechanics TBD)
- **Where they work:** Mine zone

### Smelter
- **What they do:** Processes raw materials in furnaces - ores to ingots, logs to charcoal, sand to glass, clay to bricks
- **Where they work:** Foundry zone

### Carpenter
- **What they do:** Processes wood into construction materials (planks, stairs, slabs, fences, ladders, scaffolding), crafts bows/arrows
- **Where they work:** Woodshop zone

## Compatibility Professions (COMPAT)
*These professions require specific mods to function*

### Brewer
- **What they do:** Brews drinks and potions
- **Where they work:** Brewery zone
- **Mod:** Let's Do: Vinery, Let's Do: Brewery, or Brewin' and Chewin'

### Miller
- **What they do:** Grinds wheat into flour at windmills/watermills
- **Where they work:** Mill zone
- **Mod:** Create

## Potential Professions (MAYBE)

### Builder
- **What they do:** Constructs buildings from player-created schematics using provided materials
- **Where they work:** Construction sites designated by placed schematics

### Cleric
- **What they do:** Performs marriages, conducts funerals, counsels depressed villagers, maintains cemetery
- **Where they work:** Church/chapel zone, cemetery zone

### Glassblower
- **What they do:** Creates decorative glass items, bottles, stained glass
- **Where they work:** Glassworks zone

### Herbalist
- **What they do:** Heals injured/sick villagers, grows medicinal plants, reduces recovery times
- **Where they work:** Clinic zone

### Messenger
- **What they do:** Brings time-sensitive quests from distant villages, may evolve into tax collector
- **Where they work:** Town Hall zone

### Potter
- **What they do:** Creates clay pots, decorative terracotta, ceramic items
- **Where they work:** Kiln zone

### Warden
- **What they do:** Patrols chunks to suppress hostile mob spawning temporarily
- **Where they work:** Throughout village bounds

### Scribe
- **What they do:** Copies maps and books, creates new books, potentially other document-related tasks
- **Where they work:** TBD

### Steward
- **What they do:** Organizes storage, delivers resources to crafters, tracks inventory for player requests
- **Where they work:** Storage zone

### Peddler
- **What they do:** Visits periodically with trades from distant lands (improving on vanilla's wandering trader)
- **Where they work:** Town square/market when visiting

### Merchant
- **What they do:** Sells village surplus goods for emeralds (includes specialized types like fishmonger, produce merchant, etc.)
- **Where they work:** Market Stall zone

### Weaver
- **What they do:** Creates cloth items, banners, wool armor for climate adaptation
- **Where they work:** Weaving zone

## Rejected Professions (NO)

### Cartographer
- **What they do:** In vanilla, makes maps. Could theoretically explore and map surroundings.
- **Why rejected:** Cool concept but feature creep. Maybe revisit in future.

### Librarian
- **What they do:** In vanilla, trades enchanted books. No clear role in production-based village.
- **Why rejected:** No meaningful day-to-day job. Woodworker can make paper/books.

### Night Watchman
- **What they do:** Would patrol only at night, maintaining torches
- **Why rejected:** Players can set schedules for regular Guards instead

### Prospector
- **What they do:** Would scout for ore deposits to assist miners
- **Why rejected:** Unnecessary complexity, miners can handle ore finding

### Town Crier
- **What they do:** Would announce village events
- **Why rejected:** Not enough meaningful gameplay value
